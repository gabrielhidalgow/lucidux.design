# Brand Voice & Messaging

## Five Core Attributes

### 1. Clear and Direct

**Do:**
- "Your AI works. Your interface doesn't."
- "Polished UI isn't effective UI."
- "I spot 5 problems in 30 seconds. Full audit finds 10-15."
- "Your spacing is off. Your hierarchy is flat. Let's fix both."
- "Shipped fast. Time to convert."

**Don't:**
- "We leverage synergistic UX/UI paradigms..."

---

### 2. Expert but Humble

**Do:**
- "I've evaluated 100+ AI interfaces. Same issues appear in 80%."
- "Expert judgment spots problems. Data validates they matter."

**Don't:**
- "I'm the world's best designer."

---

### 3. Technical but Human

**Do:**
- "AI generates components. Expert designers compose them effectively."
- "Visual weight distribution creates cognitive load."

**Don't:**
- "Leveraging visual paradigms to optimize aesthetic vectors."

---

### 4. Data-Informed

**Do:**
- "Expert review found 6 issues. Analytics showed 60% missed the CTA."
- "Testing confirmed: task completion improved from 47% to 91%."

**Don't:**
- "This looks better." (Says who? Measured how?)

---

### 5. Founder-Empathetic

**Do:**
- "Lovable got you 80% there fast. Expert eyes get you to 100%."
- "AI tools are smart for speed. Designer expertise is smart for effectiveness."

**Don't:**
- "You should have hired a designer from the start."

---

## Key Messaging

### Primary Message
"Clear UX/UI thinking for AI products—expert judgment validated by data."

### Supporting Messages
- "Expert designer identifies what AI tools miss—instantly."
- "Systematic methodology ensures nothing overlooked."
- "Data validation proves improvements work."
- "Polished UI isn't effective UI. Expert eyes spot the difference."

### Proof Points
- "Spot 5-7 UI issues in 30-second expert review"
- "10-15 total issues found in comprehensive audit"
- "50+ design heuristics evaluated systematically"
- "30-50% average improvement in task completion"
- "100+ AI-generated interfaces analyzed"
